Projects

One-Armed Warrior

An up-and-coming indie game title under development by a small team of programmers, artists, and musicians under the banner of Bat Byte Games, an indie game studio of which I am the founder. The game is a turn-based dungeon crawler roguelite with a retro 16-bit aesthetic and follows an old worn adventurer who saves the realm from a frightful demon lord. In terms of gameplay, One-Handed Warrior focuses on turn based strategy, resource management and decision making, with a large degree of replayability. Lastly, aesthetically, the game boasts a classic 16-bit aesthetic with exciting music, beautifully animated sprites and crunchy sound effects.
